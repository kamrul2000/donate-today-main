Our Donate Plan

Our donation plan offers vital aid for flood-affected communities in Sunamganj, Sylhet. Through immediate relief and long-term support initiatives, we aim to provide essentials like food, water, shelter, and medical care. By working together, we can help these communities recover and rebuild their lives with resilience and hope.

Food For a Family

For food security, our donation plan aims to supply nutritious meals to families affected by the floods in Sunamganj, Sylhet.

Shelter For a Family

In terms of shelter, we focus on providing sturdy temporary shelters to protect families from the elements and ensure their safety and well-being.

Education For Child

When it comes to education, our plan centers on ensuring that every child affected by the floods has access to quality education, including school supplies and support for continued learning despite the challenges they face.
